Recursive

Metaleptic is a recursion-oriented game engine and free-to-play platform. Playing Metaleptic-style means playing games within games within games endlessly, but also forking templates and mixing ready-made elements visually or using code .

Online

The online platform contains a game hub and a browser-based game engine. Connect to your account and edit your projects, play new game sets, check out newly published assets and get creative.

Gaming

 Gaming used to be the future of playing. The Metaleptic approach to video games can be expresed in one word: combinations. Playing games within games within games is fun. It becomes creative when what you play is both about exploring existing paths between  games AND creating your own content combinations. Play your way, 

Games

As a game studio, Metaleptic started by exploring the world of game engine, from simple to complex existing tools.

. . .

Along the way, we produce thought-provoking artifacts, interactive poems, mind-bending experiences and recursive gamified ads.

. . .

Our team is international and diverse. We are currently working on our first major release: a self-explorative shamanistic adventure game in VR (France/Japan/China).

Platform

It features a free game hub, an entirely customizable and  intuitive online toolkit (GUI+IDE) enabling anyone to create content efficiently.

. . .

Our unique meta-gaming protocol enables you to combine game elements, providing documentation, examples and templates.

. . .

Besides being intuitive, our platform is to be welcoming. Creating an engine means fostering a blooming community of empowered creatives who make their dreams come true.

Mindset

The philosophy behind Metaleptic is called Psychedelic Pragmaticism, an approach dedicated to playing as brain activity.

. . .

At its core, the use of recursion as a means of materializing the utmost creative dimension of self-referenciality.

. . .

Our main goal is to provide users, players and creators with the tools to engage in a playful and enriching investigation of the limits of intuition, perception and imagination.

Metaleptic Moon

Facing Epic Steam, Google Moon, Metaleptic…

 This little poem says everything you have to know. Metaleptic Moon is a space-ad shooter, recursively an advertisement for a recursive game engine in development to be unveiled soon: the Metaleptic engine. You're a triangle with Koch flake tendencies and superpowers. You shoot META and PATA bullets in order to destroy evil ads from the gaming industry. Then, you fight the three giants. And again, until you manage going META yourself, which might happen only if you train yourself properly.

Dream Invaders

Dream Invaders is a game about human dream invasion and machine dreaming.

Although machine learning is increasingly popular, we haven't heard much about it since Google launched its alleged machine dreaming software. The real issue addressed here is the upcoming development of dream-invasive technologies by tech giants who keep our personal data under good scrutiny.

Tetristein 3D

If you like shooting robotic flying advertisements, this game is for you. Pasta Panic

Pasta Panic is a simple and rather silly game about causality.

A simple and silly game about a typical existential crisis : how do you cook your humans? It was made in the simplest possible format so as to serve its major purpose which is to deploy absurd logic into a sequential experience. Tip : clicking different colors involves choosing different paths
