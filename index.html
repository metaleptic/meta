Recursive

Metaleptic is a recursion-oriented game engine and free-to-play platform. Playing Metaleptic-style means playing games within games within games endlessly, but also forking templates and mixing ready-made elements visually or using code .

Online

The online platform contains a game hub and a browser-based game engine. Connect to your account and edit your projects, play new game sets, check out newly published assets and get creative.

Gaming

 Gaming used to be the future of playing. The Metaleptic approach to video games can be expresed in one word: combinations. Playing games within games within games is fun. It becomes creative when what you play is both about exploring existing paths between  games AND creating your own content combinations. Play your way,
