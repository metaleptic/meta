Metaleptic Moon

Facing Epic Steam, Google Moon, Metaleptic…

 This little poem says everything you have to know. Metaleptic Moon is a space-ad shooter, recursively an advertisement for a recursive game engine in development to be unveiled soon: the Metaleptic engine. You're a triangle with Koch flake tendencies and superpowers. You shoot META and PATA bullets in order to destroy evil ads from the gaming industry. Then, you fight the three giants. And again, until you manage going META yourself, which might happen only if you train yourself properly.

Dream Invaders

Dream Invaders is a game about human dream invasion and machine dreaming.

Although machine learning is increasingly popular, we haven't heard much about it since Google launched its alleged machine dreaming software. The real issue addressed here is the upcoming development of dream-invasive technologies by tech giants who keep our personal data under good scrutiny.

Tetristein 3D

Create your own hellish maze by playing Tetris. (in development)

If you like shooting robotic flying advertisements, this game is for you. Actually this is but a mock-up version of a project in development : a maze game where you build your map by playing a Tetris game in the classic style of post-contemporary recursion-oriented Metalepsis. You'll get the point.

Pasta Panic

Pasta Panic is a simple and rather silly game about causality.

A simple and silly game about a typical existential crisis : how do you cook your humans? It was made in the simplest possible format so as to serve its major purpose which is to deploy absurd logic into a sequential experience. Tip : clicking different colors involves choosing different paths
